INDEMNIFICATION AGREEMENT

This Indemnification Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of July 14, 2026, by and between Sterling & Associates ("Firm") and Client Name ("Client").

1. SCOPE OF INDEMNIFICATION

1.1 The Firm agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Client from any and all claims, damages, losses, liabilities, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or in connection with the Firm's performance of legal services under this Agreement.

1.2 Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Firm shall not be liable for any claims arising from the Client's own negligence, willful misconduct, or material breach of this Agreement.

2. INDEMNIFICATION BASKET

2.1 The Firm's indemnification obligations shall only apply when aggregate claims exceed five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) (the "Basket").

2.2 Once the Basket is exceeded, the Firm shall indemnify the Client for the full amount of all claims from the first dollar, subject to the Cap defined in Section 3.

3. LIABILITY CAP

3.1 The Firm's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed three times (3x) the total fees paid by the Client to the Firm in the twelve (12) months preceding the first claim (the "Cap").

3.2 The Cap shall not apply to claims arising from the Firm's fraud, gross negligence, willful misconduct, or infringement of the Client's intellectual property rights.

4. TERMINATION

4.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement upon sixty (60) days written notice to the other party.

4.2 In the event of a material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice.

4.3 Upon termination, the Firm shall complete all pending matters and return all Client documents and property within thirty (30) days.

5. GOVERNING LAW

5.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.

5.2 Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in New York County, New York.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary and confidential information exchanged during the course of this engagement.

6.2 This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
